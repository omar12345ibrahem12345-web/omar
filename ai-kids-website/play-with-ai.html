🛡️ Stay Safe with AI

👨‍👩‍👧‍👦

Ask a Grown-up First

Always ask a parent or teacher before trying new AI tools online. They'll help you find safe, kid-friendly ones!

🔒

Don't Share Secrets

Never tell AI your real name, address, phone number, school, or passwords. Keep private stuff private!

🤔

AI Can Be Wrong

AI makes mistakes! It might give wrong answers or make things up. Always double-check with a grown-up or trusted source.

😊

Be Kind to AI

Treat AI nicely - it's learning just like you! Being polite helps AI give better answers, plus it's just nice to be kind.
